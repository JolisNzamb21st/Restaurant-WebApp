About Restaurant

Welcome to Acacia Hotel Mbarara Fine Dining Restaurant!Located within the serene Acacia Hotel Mbarara, our restaurant offers a peaceful and well-organized dining experience surrounded by vibrant flowers and calming greenery. Known for “Service with Difference,” we take pride in warm hospitality and exceptional service. Whether you're here for a quiet meal or a casual gathering, our space is designed for comfort, flavor, and peace of mind.

Founded in 2016, The Acacia Hotels Africa is a prominent player in the hospitality industry, known for its commitment to providing luxurious accommodations and exceptional service. With a vision to create memorable experiences for guests,the brand has rapidly expanded its footprint, establishing a strong presence in both Uganda and Africa at large.
